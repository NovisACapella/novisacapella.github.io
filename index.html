This is Novis.

We're the premier A Cappella group at the University of Texas at Dallas.

In light of the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, Novis is not currently holding or planning any in-person events. Rehearsals and recorded performances are being conducted virtually. We extend our heartfelt appreciation to those performing essential work during this time, as well as our deepest sympathies to everyone who has been negatively impacted by the current global situation.
