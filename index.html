This is Novis.

We're the premier A Cappella group at the University of Texas at Dallas.

We have returned to in-person rehearsals and events, but the COVID-19 pandemic is not over. We highly encourage our members and audiences to both wear masks and get vaccinated.

About

Novis A Cappella has been delighting UTD audiences with unaccompanied vocal performances for over a decade. Established in 2009, the group focuses on both musical excellence and cultivating friendships among its members. A typical semester in Novis begins with learning big-group pieces. Short performances and small-group music practice come next, then preparation for our major concert, which typically takes place at Jonsson Performance Hall.
