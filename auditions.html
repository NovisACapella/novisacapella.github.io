How to become a Novite:

  1. Fill out the form below
  2. Prepare a minute of your favorite song to sing for us
  3. Sing it at your chosen time
  4. Do a little sight-reading for us
  5. Learn a short excerpt and sing with the officers
  6. Celebrate!
