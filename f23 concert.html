Come join us for an evening of a cappella perfection featuring our renditions of popular love songs! Doors open at 7:00pm; no tickets necessary!

For more information about our organization, including our member roster, visit our main site page.

Please remember to silence your cell phone.

Can't make it in person? Join our livestream!
